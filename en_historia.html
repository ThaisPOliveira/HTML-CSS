SPAIN



Spain is a country located on the western European continent. It is developed and industrialized. It concentrates a very rich culture, with several tourist centers.

Located on the Iberian Peninsula, which lies between the Mediterranean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean. The country had great historical importance, being one of the greatest kingdoms of antiquity, especially in the great navigations. It colonized several lands, which today carry a little of its history.

Tourism, agriculture and industry are the main sources of income in the country. A place with a stable economy and where the population has good living conditions.

History of Spain




The Spanish territory has been inhabited since antiquity by different populations, such as Moors, Arabs and Romans, who spread across several kingdoms. The unification of Spain could only be achieved through political and economic agreements that permeated the monarchy in the region. The Church also played a powerful role as the country was unified after the adoption of Catholicism as the official religion.



The Puerta de Alcalá is one of the main historic landmarks of the city of Madrid.



After unification, Spain became one of the greatest powers in the world, especially due to its protagonist during the Great Navigations. It owns most of the colonized lands, mainly in the Americas, especially in most Latin American countries. Internal problems and external upheavals led to economic recession, which led to the loss of their colonies.

In the last century, Spain underwent an important political change, as it was ruled by a dictatorship for almost 40 years. The Spanish monarchy is back in power after dark years, restoring the country's democracy. Soon, Spain recovered politically and economically, becoming one of the most developed countries in the world.
