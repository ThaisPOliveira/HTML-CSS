SPAIN HYMN


The anthem of Spain, called Marcha Real, is one of the oldest hymns in Europe and first appeared in 1761.
It does not have a lyric, but there have been several attempts to insert it.
The last attempt was created in 1928, called “Viva España“, but at the end of Francisco Franco's dictatorship, this version was abandoned by the Spanish population.
